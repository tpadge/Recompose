Welcome to recompose

Modern classical music is evolving. The great composers of today are reimagining the great compositions of yesterday, taking timeless repetoire to a new, modern setting.

Modern technology and recording allows today's composers a greater scope of composition, expanding the texture, instrumentation, and sound of their predecessors.

Recompose is made to enhance your listening experience. Select a modern or traditional setting of your favorite work, then use the playback controls to view an audio visualization of each piece. Additionally, view program notes to enhance your understanding of each setting.
